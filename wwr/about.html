History

Water rafting has been around for centuries, with evidence of early river travel found in ancient Egyptian, Babylonian, and Greek civilizations. In the mid-1800s, people began to see water rafting as a recreational activity.

In ancient times, water rafting was primarily used for transportation, trade, and warfare. It wasn't until the early 1900s that the first commercial river trips began, offering tourists a chance to experience the excitement of white water rafting.
